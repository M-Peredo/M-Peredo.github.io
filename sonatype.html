Sonatype Marketing Campaign

Sonatype Marketing Campaign

Marketing Campaign | Website Design | Exhibit & Trade Show Design

Organization: Sonatype

Roles: Graphic Designer

Sonatype’s software platforms allow digital developers to accelerate innovation and improve application security. As part of their marketing efforts, they develop themed educational campaigns to teach developers about the security issues involved with the use of open source software components. This campaign was called Raise the B.A.R.R. and used a Star Wars inspired style to highlight different categories of software components that should be avoided.
